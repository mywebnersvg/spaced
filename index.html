THE MOON!

More than just a destination

Our newest destination is the shortest trip SPACED offers. You will have great views of earth, perfect for taking pictures. Not only will you have a luxury experience getting to the moon, you will also be over the moon for the accommodations we have for you on earth’s natural satellite. Be sure to look into our events held on the moon, you wont want to miss the opportunity to see the next eclipse from the moons surface.

The Flight

What to expect onboard

The flight to our destinations can get long, but you wont be sitting in a small seat like the airlines you are used to. With SPACED you will enjoy a smooth launch and a quick trip to our interstellar luxury starship. For your stay onboard you can enjoy all of the amenities included butnot limited to: our galactic bar, space walking, zero gravity rec center, and our very own movie theatre. Time will fly when you are traveling to your destination. Don’t know what all to do on your trip? Let us help you planet.
